01 / Who I am

I build capability that outlasts the project.

I am an Implementation and Training Specialist with over 10 years of experience delivering complex SaaS onboarding, training programme design and long-term digital adoption across B2B clients. I build things from scratch, take full ownership, and leave people more capable than I found them.

Throughout my career I have designed and embedded training and implementation functions from scratch at multiple organisations, each time in environments where nothing formal existed, and each time the result became the recognised standard within the business or sector.

I am genuinely passionate about teaching people and get enormous satisfaction from watching others grow, develop and gain confidence. That drive to build capability in the people around me is at the heart of everything I do.
